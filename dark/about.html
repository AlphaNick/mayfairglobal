Who we are

Who WE ARE?

Mayfair Global Consulting (MGC) is a leading global financial and professional services firm.

Our reach, experience and services are as wide, rich and deep as we are diverse. Our elite teams are among the most experienced in their particular skill set and their industry. Indeed, MGC’s Founder has personally provided international services for over 40 years in jurisdictions as wide-ranging as Monaco, Barbados, Cyprus, Switzerland and of course the United Kingdom.

Our advisors are highly qualified and exceptionally skilled at what they do, typically exercising over 20 years’ individual experience. Our team has been delivering a discreet and effective financial and professional service for many years and welcomes new clients looking to preserve wealth and enjoy financial freedom.

Our Clientele

MGC is a corporation that offers a total turn-key approach for its clients who typically, but not exclusively, live and work overseas, or intend to in the not too distant future.

Our clients span most professions, industries and regions, and primarily include, but are not limited to, high net-worth individuals & families, businesses and governments. Our success has always been built on providing the very best solutions to securing a healthy, wealthy and successful future, thereby enabling our clients to reach their full potential.

We guard our track-record jealously and we pride ourselves on developing our business to make life easier and more fulfilling for our clients.

As diverse as our clientele is, all of our clients want to succeed in their endeavours just as we wish to succeed in ours.

Working with MGC is an enriching experience and our elite team work hard to build and maintain a long-term relationship with each client.
